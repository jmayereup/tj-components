Progressive Language Placement Test Demo

🔑 Start Code: 1234 | 🔒 Teacher Code: 7676
ℹ️ Progressive Mechanics: Students answer section by section. Meeting or exceeding the pass threshold (e.g. 70%–80%) unlocks the next section. If a student fails to meet the cutoff, the test stops and their placement level is determined.
